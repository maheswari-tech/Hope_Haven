Vision and Mission

VISION

To create a hunger-free world where every surplus meal finds its way to a child in need, starting with orphanages in our local communities.

MISSION

We use tech to match surplus food with orphanages, empower giving, raise awareness, and ensure no meal is wasted.

VALUES

We believe every human deserves dignity, care, and a warm meal. Our work starts with empathy.
